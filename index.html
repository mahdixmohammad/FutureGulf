About Us

Our History:

Based in Iraq, this company was established in 2011 and registered in 2019 in the Ministry of Commerce of Iraq. Currently, our franchises are situated in Karbala and Najaf. ICE PACK marked the beginning of our company's journey into franchising.

Who Are We?

A leading company in the Iraqi Market, we own several brands that possess contemporary ideas, and provides fantastic job opportunity for local citizens. The company is distinguished by its unique and talented team and has recruited more than 60 employees across all franchises. The company aims to meet customers’ needs through providing creative constant services in the local market. Our agendas include manufacturing and marketing food products, juice brands, ice cream flavors and cooking products according to the best international quality standards and modern methods used in fields of production, filling, and packing. Our management endeavors to offer a comprehensive range of amenities, ensuring the procurement of top-tier products that are promptly dispatched to our customers' doorsteps through an efficient and swift delivery network.

Our Vision:

We are consistently exploring opportunities to partner with world-renowned franchises, aiming to create additional job prospects while delivering top-notch quality and service to our valued customers, all while ensuring competitive pricing. We aim to create an atmosphere of collaborative spirit among the employees whiling pushing the creative aspect of the company and outlining innovative strategies from management.
